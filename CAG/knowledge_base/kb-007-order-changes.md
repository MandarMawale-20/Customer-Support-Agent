# Changing an Order After Placement

**Article ID:** kb-007
**Last updated:** 2025-09-22

Once an order is placed, your ability to change it depends on how far along it is.

## Within 15 minutes of ordering

You can edit most things directly from your order page: shipping address, items, size, colour. Look for the **Edit order** button.

## After 15 minutes, before shipping

Self-edit is no longer available. Contact support with your order number and what you'd like to change. We can usually still update:

- **Shipping address** — yes, as long as the new address is in the same country as the original.
- **Shipping method** — yes, with a price adjustment if applicable.
- **Item swaps** — usually no. Cancel and re-order is the cleaner path (see *Cancelling an Order*).

## After shipping

The order is in the carrier's hands and we cannot intervene. For address changes after shipping, most carriers offer a "redirect to pickup point" option directly in their tracking page — try that first.

## Special case: gift orders

For orders flagged as gifts, the recipient (not the buyer) can also request address changes provided they have the order number.

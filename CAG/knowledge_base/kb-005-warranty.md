# Warranty Claims

**Article ID:** kb-005
**Last updated:** 2025-07-20

Most of our products come with a standard **2-year manufacturer warranty** covering defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty starts on the **date of delivery**.

## What's covered

- Manufacturing defects that appear under normal use.
- Component failures not caused by user damage.
- Stitching, seam, or assembly issues on clothing and bags.

## What's not covered

- Damage from misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear.
- Damage from unauthorised repairs or modifications.
- Cosmetic wear that does not affect function.
- Items purchased from unauthorised resellers.

## Extended warranty

Select electronics offer a paid extended warranty (3 or 5 years total) purchasable at checkout or within 30 days of delivery. Check the product page for eligibility.

## How to file a warranty claim

1. Gather your **order number** and a description of the fault, plus photos or a short video if relevant.
2. Contact support with the details.
3. We'll review and, if approved, arrange one of:
   - **Repair** at our service centre (free shipping both ways within the EU).
   - **Replacement** if repair is not possible.
   - **Refund** at our discretion if neither repair nor replacement is viable.

Warranty review typically takes 3–5 business days. Repair turnaround is 2–4 weeks once the item is received.

## Proof of purchase

You'll need your order number or a copy of the order confirmation email. Warranty claims without proof of purchase cannot be processed.

# International Shipping & Customs

**Article ID:** kb-011
**Last updated:** 2025-08-01

## Where we ship

We ship to most countries worldwide. A small number of destinations are restricted due to carrier limitations or local regulations — these will show as unavailable at checkout.

## Duties and import taxes

For orders shipped **outside the EU**, you may be charged **import duties, VAT, or handling fees** by the destination country. These are:

- **Set by the destination country**, not by us.
- **Paid by the recipient**, typically to the carrier on delivery or via a pre-payment link.
- **Not refundable by us** if the order is returned or refused.

For UK orders, the carrier will usually contact you before delivery to collect any duties owed.

## DDP vs. DDU

Most of our international shipments are **DDU** (Delivered Duty Unpaid): you pay duties on arrival. A small number of high-volume corridors are **DDP** (Delivered Duty Paid): duties collected at checkout and forwarded by us. The product page and checkout will indicate which applies to your destination.

## Refused or unclaimed parcels

If you refuse delivery, ignore a customs notification, or fail to pay duties, the parcel will be returned to us. We will refund the **product cost** once received, but **not the original shipping fee** and **not any duties already incurred**. Return shipping fees from the destination country may also be deducted.

## Restricted items

Some items cannot be shipped to certain countries (e.g. lithium batteries, certain cosmetics). These will not be available for purchase at checkout when you select a restricted destination.

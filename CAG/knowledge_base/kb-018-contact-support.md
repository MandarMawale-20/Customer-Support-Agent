# Contacting Support

**Article ID:** kb-018
**Last updated:** 2025-09-29

## Channels

- **Email:** support@example.com — response within one business day.
- **Live chat:** available from the help page, weekdays 09:00–18:00 CET.
- **Help centre:** browse articles 24/7 — most questions are answered there.

We do not offer phone support.

## What to include when you contact us

To help us resolve quickly:

- Your **order number** (starts with `ORD-`).
- The **email address** the order was placed with.
- A clear description of the issue.
- Photos or screenshots if relevant (damaged item, error message, etc.).

## Response times

- **Standard queries:** within 1 business day.
- **Urgent issues** (damaged item, delivery problem): we aim to respond within a few hours during business time.
- **Refund / financial requests:** these may take longer (up to 2 business days) because a human reviews each one.

## Escalation

If you've been in a thread for more than 3 business days without resolution, reply with the word **"escalate"** and a senior team member will pick it up within 1 business day.

## Languages

We provide support in English, Dutch, German, and French. Replies in other languages may be machine-assisted.

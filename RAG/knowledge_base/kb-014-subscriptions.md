# Managing Subscriptions

**Article ID:** kb-014
**Last updated:** 2025-09-18

A subscription auto-ships a product to you on a recurring schedule (monthly, every 2 months, or every 3 months) at a 10% discount compared to one-off orders.

## Managing your subscription

Go to **Account → Subscriptions** to:

- See your next ship date.
- Change the cadence.
- Skip the next shipment.
- Pause for up to 3 months.
- Update the shipping address or payment method.
- Cancel.

## Cancellation

You can cancel anytime from the subscription page. Cancellation takes effect immediately — you will **not** be charged for or shipped any further orders. We do not require a phone call or a reason.

If a renewal has already been processed (you've been charged but the order has not yet shipped), you can either:

- Let it ship and return it under the standard return policy, or
- Contact support quickly to attempt to stop the shipment.

## Renewal notifications

We email you **3 days before** each renewal charges. You can change or skip the upcoming shipment up until the renewal processes.

## Failed renewals

If your payment method fails on renewal, we retry for 5 days. After 5 days of failure, the subscription is paused. You can reactivate by updating your payment method.

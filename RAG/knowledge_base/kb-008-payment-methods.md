# Payment Methods

**Article ID:** kb-008
**Last updated:** 2025-08-15

We accept:

- **Credit and debit cards:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **iDEAL** (Netherlands)
- **Bancontact** (Belgium)
- **PayPal**
- **Klarna** — Pay Now, Pay in 30, or Pay in 3
- **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay**
- **Gift cards** issued by us
- **Bank transfer** (manual; orders ship only after the transfer clears, typically 1–3 business days)

All transactions are processed in EUR. If your card is denominated in another currency, your bank will convert at its current rate.

## When you're charged

- Card / wallet payments: your card is authorised at checkout and **charged when the order ships**.
- iDEAL / Bancontact: charged immediately at checkout.
- Klarna Pay in 30 / Pay in 3: per Klarna's own schedule; we do not control this.
- Bank transfer: charged when you initiate the transfer; order ships when funds clear.

## Declined payments

If your payment is declined, we will notify you by email and hold the order for 24 hours so you can update payment. After 24 hours, the order is cancelled and any items released back to inventory.

## Saved cards

You can save card details to your account for faster checkout. We use a PCI-compliant payment processor; we do not store full card numbers on our servers. Saved cards can be removed at any time from **Account → Payment methods**.

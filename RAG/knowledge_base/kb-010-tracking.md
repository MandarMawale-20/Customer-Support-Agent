# Tracking Your Order

**Article ID:** kb-010
**Last updated:** 2025-09-12

## Where to find your tracking number

Your tracking number is included in the **shipping confirmation email** sent when your order leaves the warehouse. You can also find it under **Orders → [your order] → Tracking** when signed in.

## When tracking goes live

Tracking numbers usually become active in the carrier's system **within a few hours** of being issued, but can take up to 24 hours. A tracking number that returns "not found" right after you receive it is normal — wait a day.

## Tracking hasn't moved for several days

A tracking page that hasn't updated for 2–3 business days is usually fine — carriers often scan parcels only at major hubs.

If tracking shows **no movement for 5+ business days** past the original estimated delivery date, the parcel may be lost. Contact us and we'll open an investigation with the carrier. Most "lost" parcels are recovered within 7 days; if not, we will issue a replacement or refund.

## Tracking shows "delivered" but I didn't get it

Take these steps in order:

1. Check with neighbours and household members.
2. Look in safe spots: porch, side gate, behind plants.
3. Check the carrier's photo proof of delivery if available.
4. Wait 24 hours — occasionally couriers mark parcels delivered prematurely.

If after all of the above you still haven't found it, contact us. We will open a claim with the carrier; resolution typically takes 5–10 business days.

## International tracking

For Rest-of-World shipments, tracking may go dark for several days while the parcel is in transit between carriers (e.g. between our courier and your local post). This is normal.

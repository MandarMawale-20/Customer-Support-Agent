# Refunds & Processing Times

**Article ID:** kb-003
**Last updated:** 2025-09-01

## When refunds are issued

A refund is issued once your return has been **received and inspected** at our warehouse. You'll get an email confirmation as soon as the refund is processed on our side.

## How long it takes to land

The time from refund issued to money in your account depends on the payment method:

| Method | Time to land |
|---|---|
| Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) | 3–10 business days |
| Debit card | 3–10 business days |
| iDEAL | 1–3 business days |
| PayPal | 1–2 business days |
| Klarna / Pay Later | Refund applied to your Klarna balance within 3 business days |
| Bank transfer | 5–10 business days |
| Gift card | Immediately, as store credit |

If it's been longer than the window above, check with your bank first — they sometimes hold refunds for a few extra days. If your bank confirms there's nothing pending, contact us with your order number.

## Partial refunds

We may issue a partial refund if:

- Items are returned with signs of wear or use.
- Original packaging or tags are missing.
- The return arrives after the 30-day window.

The partial amount is at our discretion and will be explained in the refund email.

## Refunds for items damaged in transit

If your order arrived damaged, you do not need to return it before we refund or replace. See *Damaged or Faulty Items*.

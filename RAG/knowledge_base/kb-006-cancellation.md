# Cancelling an Order

**Article ID:** kb-006
**Last updated:** 2025-09-22

## Before your order ships

You can cancel an order yourself **as long as it has not yet shipped**:

1. Sign in and go to **Orders**.
2. Find the order and click **Cancel order**.
3. You'll see a confirmation, and a refund is issued immediately to your original payment method.

If the **Cancel order** button is no longer visible, the order has already entered our warehouse picking process and cannot be cancelled by you directly. Contact support quickly — we may still be able to stop it.

## After your order ships

Once a tracking number is generated, the order **cannot be cancelled**. Your options are:

- **Refuse delivery** at the door — the carrier will return the parcel to us and we'll refund you once it arrives (3–10 business days after the parcel reaches us).
- **Accept and return** under the standard return policy (see *Return Policy*).

## Partial cancellations

You can cancel individual items from a multi-item order using the same flow, provided the order has not shipped yet. The remaining items will ship as normal and you'll be refunded only for the cancelled items.

## Refund timing on cancellations

Cancellation refunds use the same processing times as other refunds. See *Refunds & Processing Times*.

## Cancelling a subscription

For subscription products (e.g. monthly consumables), see *Managing Subscriptions*.

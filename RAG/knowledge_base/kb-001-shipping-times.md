# Shipping Times & Delivery Estimates

**Article ID:** kb-001
**Last updated:** 2025-09-12

We ship from warehouses in Rotterdam (NL) and Łódź (PL). Estimated delivery times depend on the destination zone, not the warehouse:

| Zone | Countries | Standard | Express |
|---|---|---|---|
| EU-West | NL, BE, DE, FR, LU | 2–4 business days | 1–2 business days |
| EU-South | ES, IT, PT, GR | 4–6 business days | 2–3 business days |
| EU-North | DK, SE, FI, NO | 3–5 business days | 2–3 business days |
| UK | UK | 4–7 business days | 2–4 business days |
| Rest of World | Everywhere else | 7–14 business days | 4–7 business days |

Delivery estimates start counting **once the order ships**, not at order time. Most orders ship within 1 business day of being placed. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays ship the next business day.

Express shipping is available at checkout for an additional fee. Standard is free on orders over €40.

If your tracking link shows no movement for 5+ business days past the estimated delivery date, please contact us — the carrier may have lost the parcel.

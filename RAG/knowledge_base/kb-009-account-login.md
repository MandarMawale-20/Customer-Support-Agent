# Account & Login Help

**Article ID:** kb-009
**Last updated:** 2025-09-05

## Resetting your password

1. Go to the sign-in page and click **Forgot password**.
2. Enter the email address on your account.
3. We'll send a reset link valid for 30 minutes.

If the email doesn't arrive within 5 minutes:

- Check your spam folder.
- Make sure you're using the email address the account was created with.
- Try requesting the link again — old links are invalidated when a new one is requested.

## Email not recognised

If we tell you the email is not on file, either:

- You signed up with a different email address (try common alternatives you use), **or**
- You checked out as a guest and don't actually have an account. In that case you can still look up your order using the order confirmation email and the order number — no account needed.

## Changing your email address

You can change the email on file from **Account → Profile**. We'll send a verification link to the new address; the change takes effect once you click that link.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

2FA is optional and can be enabled from **Account → Security**. We support authenticator apps (TOTP). SMS-based 2FA is **not** supported, for security reasons.

## Deleting your account

You can request account deletion from **Account → Privacy**. Deletion is irreversible and processed within 30 days. Order history is retained for tax purposes per local law, but personal data is removed.

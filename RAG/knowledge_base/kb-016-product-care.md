# Product Care & Maintenance

**Article ID:** kb-016
**Last updated:** 2025-05-22

General care guidance by product category. Always check the **care label** on the individual product first — it overrides anything here.

## Clothing

- Wash inside-out at 30°C or lower for most fabrics.
- Avoid tumble drying where possible; reshape and hang to dry.
- Iron on the appropriate setting for the fabric — start low and increase if needed.
- Wash dark colours separately for the first 2–3 washes.

## Knitwear

- Hand wash or use a gentle wool cycle.
- Dry flat — hanging can stretch the shoulders.
- Store folded, not hung, to maintain shape.

## Leather goods

- Wipe with a soft dry cloth after use.
- Condition every 3–6 months with a leather-safe conditioner.
- Avoid prolonged sun exposure and direct heat.
- If the item gets wet, let it air-dry away from heat sources, then condition.

## Electronics

- Charge with the supplied cable and a quality adapter.
- Avoid extreme temperatures (below 0°C or above 40°C).
- For products with batteries, store at around 50% charge if not used for long periods.

## Home goods (ceramics, glass, wood)

- Most ceramics and glassware are dishwasher safe unless stated otherwise on the product page.
- Wood items should be hand-washed and oiled occasionally with a food-safe oil if used with food.

If a care label is missing or unclear, contact support with the product name and we'll send you specific care instructions.

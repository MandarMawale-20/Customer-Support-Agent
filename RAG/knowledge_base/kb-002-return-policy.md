# Return Policy

**Article ID:** kb-002
**Last updated:** 2025-08-30

You can return most items within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund, no questions asked.

## What can be returned

- Unworn clothing and accessories with original tags attached.
- Electronics in their original packaging, with all included accessories.
- Home goods in unused, resaleable condition.

## What cannot be returned

- Items marked "Final Sale" on the product page.
- Personalised or custom-made items (e.g. engraved items, made-to-measure clothing).
- Opened consumables (cosmetics, food, supplements) for hygiene reasons.
- Gift cards.

## How to start a return

1. Sign in to your account and go to **Orders → Return an item**.
2. Select the item(s) you want to return and a reason.
3. Print the prepaid return label we email you.
4. Drop the package at any partner pickup point within 14 days of starting the return.

Returns are free for customers in the EU. UK and Rest-of-World customers pay return shipping.

## When you'll get your refund

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** within 5–10 business days of us receiving the return. See *Refunds & Processing Times* for details.

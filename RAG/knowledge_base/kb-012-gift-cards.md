# Gift Cards

**Article ID:** kb-012
**Last updated:** 2025-06-30

## Buying a gift card

Gift cards are available in denominations from **€10 to €500**. You can choose to:

- Send the gift card by email immediately or on a scheduled date.
- Have it sent to yourself to forward later.

Gift cards are delivered as a digital code with an optional personal message. We do not ship physical gift cards.

## Using a gift card

Enter the code at checkout in the **Promo / Gift card** field. The card's balance will be deducted from your order total. If the order is less than the card's value, the remaining balance stays on the card for future use.

## Checking your balance

Sign in and go to **Account → Gift cards**, or use the balance lookup link on the gift-card landing page (no sign-in required).

## Expiry

Gift cards are valid for **5 years from the purchase date**.

## Lost or stolen gift cards

Gift cards work like cash — if the code is shared or intercepted, anyone with the code can use it. We cannot replace a lost code unless it has not yet been redeemed and you have proof of purchase. Contact support immediately if you believe a code has been compromised.

## Refunds and gift cards

- Gift cards themselves are **non-refundable** once delivered.
- If you paid for an order partly with a gift card and partly with another method, refunds are applied to the **gift card first**, then to the other method.

# Store Credit

**Article ID:** kb-017
**Last updated:** 2025-09-01

Store credit is balance held on your account that can be used toward future orders.

## How you get store credit

- As a goodwill resolution offered by support (we may offer this in place of a refund in specific situations).
- As compensation for service issues — for example, a significantly delayed order.
- As a refund of a payment method we can no longer credit (e.g. a closed bank card more than 60 days after the original order).

We do not sell store credit directly. If you want to pre-load value to your account, buy a **gift card** instead — see *Gift Cards*.

## Using store credit

Store credit is applied automatically at checkout when you're signed in, **before** any other payment method. You'll see it as a line in your order summary.

You can choose to **not use** available credit by toggling it off in the order summary.

## Expiry

Store credit issued by support has **no expiry**. Store credit issued as a return-window-exceeded refund or a partial-refund makegood is valid for **24 months** from issue.

## Refunds vs. store credit

Customers can always request a **refund to the original payment method** instead of store credit, except in cases where the original payment method is no longer reachable (closed card, expired PayPal account, etc.). Any agent offering store credit should make clear it's an option, not a requirement.

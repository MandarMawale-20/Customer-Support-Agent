# Damaged or Faulty Items

**Article ID:** kb-004
**Last updated:** 2025-09-15

If your order arrives damaged, broken, or faulty, we will make it right at no cost to you.

## What to do

1. **Take photos** of the damaged item and the packaging within 48 hours of delivery.
2. Contact support with your order number and the photos attached.
3. We will offer you a choice:
   - A **replacement** of the same item, shipped at our expense.
   - A **full refund** to the original payment method.

You do **not** need to return the damaged item before we resolve. We may ask you to dispose of it responsibly or, occasionally, to ship it back with a prepaid label for quality inspection.

## What counts as "damaged or faulty"

- Item arrived broken, cracked, or with visible damage.
- Item is missing parts that the product page listed as included.
- Item does not function as described on first use.

## Time limit

Damage claims must be filed within **14 days of delivery**. Beyond that, the item is treated as a standard return (see *Return Policy*) and may be subject to the partial-refund terms.

## Faults that appear later

For items that develop a fault after the first 14 days but within the warranty period, see *Warranty Claims*.
